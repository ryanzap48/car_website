About the C7 Z06

Released in 2015, the Corvette C7 Z06 was designed to be the most track-capable Corvette ever built. The aggressive aerodynamics, wider stance, and unique styling set it apart from standard Corvette models.

With a 6.2L supercharged LT4 V8, it delivers an astonishing 650 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque, making it one of the most powerful production cars in the world at its release.

Available in both coupe and convertible configurations, the Z06 features an available Z07 Performance Package with Brembo carbon ceramic brakes, Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires, and adjustable aerodynamic components.
